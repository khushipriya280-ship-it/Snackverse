Tips for Beginners

  • Prevent Leaking: Make sure the balls are coated completely in breadcrumbs. If there's a gap, the cheese will melt and leak into the oil, which causes splattering and empty shells.
  • Oil Temp: Test the oil temperature by dropping in a few breadcrumbs. If they sizzle and rise immediately, the oil is ready. If they burn, the oil is too hot.
  • Make Ahead: You can shape and coat the cheese balls in advance and freeze them. Fry them directly from the freezer whenever late-night cravings strike!
