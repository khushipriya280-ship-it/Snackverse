Chinese Bhel

An incredibly addictive Indo-Chinese street food snack. Crispy deep-fried noodles tossed with crunchy julienned vegetables and coated in a glossy, sweet, spicy, and tangy sauce.

Tips for Beginners

  • Mix Last Minute: To prevent soggy noodles, do not mix the fried noodles with the sauce until you are ready to eat.
  • Air-Fryer Friendly: Spritz the boiled noodles with oil and air fry them at 190°C for 10-12 minutes, tossing frequently, for a low-oil version of crispy noodles!
  • Crunch Level: Keep vegetable slices uniform and thin (julienned) so they cook quickly but retain a fresh crunch in the bhel.
