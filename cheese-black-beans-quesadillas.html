Warm, crispy, golden-brown flour tortillas folded over a savory spiced black bean, onion, and tomato filling, glued together with a mountain of melting Cheddar cheese.

Tips for Beginners

  • Keep it Low: Toast the quesadilla on low heat. This gives the cheese enough time to melt completely before the outer tortilla burns.
  • Extra Crispy: Lightly brush the outside of the tortillas with butter or olive oil before placing them on the skillet.
  • Spice Upgrade: Toss in a few chopped pickled jalapeño slices before folding to give the quesadillas an extra spicy kick!
