Tips for Beginners

  • Whisk Well: Whisking the batter for a minute adds air, which results in a lighter, crispier outer shell.
  • Seal Completely: Ensure the cheese cube is fully enclosed by the potato mixture. If cheese is exposed, it will melt out into the pan while cooking.
  • Healthy Alternative: The paniyaram/appe pan method is a fantastic way to get crispy bondas with only a fraction of the oil used in deep frying!
