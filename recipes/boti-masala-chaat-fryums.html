Tips for Beginners

  • Keep it Crunchy: Fryums absorb moisture incredibly fast. Do not assemble the chaat until you are ready to put it in your mouth!
  • Spice Control: You can adjust the ratio of green chutney (spicy) to tamarind chutney (sweet/sour) depending on your spice tolerance.
  • Fryum Choices: You can use standard circular tube fryums or elongated finger fryums for this recipe.
