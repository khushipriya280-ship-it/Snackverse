Tips for Beginners

  • Keep Dahi Cold: Always use chilled yogurt. The combination of ice-cold sweet yogurt and warm spices on a crispy shell is what makes this dish legendary.
  • Quick Assembly: Work quickly when assembling. The puris will begin to soften once filled with liquid yogurt and chutneys.
  • One Bite rule: Do not try to bite a Dahi Puri in half! It will result in a major mess. Pop the entire puri in your mouth at once.
