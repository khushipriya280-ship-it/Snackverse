Beef Birria Tacos

Melt-in-your-mouth slow-cooked beef in a rich, spiced chili broth, folded into corn tortillas with gooey cheese and fried until crispy.

Cook Time 45 Mins

Tips for Beginners

  • Dip the Tortilla: Dipping the corn tortilla in the fat layer at the top of the consomme before frying is critical. It infuses flavor and helps the shell crisp up beautifully.
  • Keep it Smooth: If your blender doesn't get the chili paste 100% smooth, strain the paste through a mesh sieve before pouring it over the beef to avoid tough skins in your broth.
  • Cheese is Key: Authentic Birria uses Oaxaca cheese, but low-moisture mozzarella or Monterey Jack will give you the same glorious, gooey stretch.
