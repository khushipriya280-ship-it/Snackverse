Chicken Kathi Roll

A classic Kolkata street-style wrap. A flaky, golden paratha coated in egg, wrapped around juicy spiced tandoori chicken tikka, crunchy onions, capsicum, and tangy chaat masala.

Prep Time 15 Mins
Cook Time 25 Mins
Difficulty Medium
Servings 4 Portions

Tips for Beginners

  • Keep Chicken Thin: Cutting chicken into thin strips ensures it cooks quickly and remains extremely tender without drying out.
  • Tawa Temperature: Ensure the tawa is hot when adding the egg, so the egg sets immediately instead of running off the edges of the paratha.
  • Pickled Onions: Squeeze lemon juice and add a pinch of salt/sugar to raw onion rings 10 minutes beforehand to make homemade pickled onions that cut through the rich flavors!
