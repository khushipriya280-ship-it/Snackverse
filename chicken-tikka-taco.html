Tips for Beginners

  • Taco Warming: Never skip warming the taco shells! Warmed shells are far more crisp and hold their shape better.
  • Vegetarian Version: Swap chicken tikka for paneer tikka or baked tofu cubes to make this recipe vegetarian.
  • Extra Punch: Squeeze a slice of lime over the tacos just before serving to brighten all the rich flavors.
