Tips for Beginners

  • Watch the Heat: Keep the pan on medium-low. Because pesto contains nuts, garlic, and cheese, it can burn or become bitter quickly if the heat is too high.
  • No Extra Oil Needed: Pesto naturally has a high olive oil content. When heated, the oil will separate and fry the eggs perfectly—no butter or cooking spray required!
  • Mind the Salt: Most store-bought pestos are already quite salty. Taste your pesto beforehand, and go light on the extra salt at the end.
